How the Queue Works

  • 1
    Login and submit your hostel application
  • 2
    Get assigned a queue number (first-come first-served)
  • 3
    Admin reviews in order and accepts or rejects
  • 4
    Select your preferred room and pay the challan
  • 5
    Room confirmed once payment is verified
